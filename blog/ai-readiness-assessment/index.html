Is Your Business Ready for AI? A 10-Question Assessment

Use this checklist to evaluate readiness and identify the best starting point for AI.

Score yourself (1-5)

We have at least one workflow with a clear bottleneck Our team uses consistent templates or SOPs We track basic KPIs for key workflows We have a tool owner or internal champion Our data is reasonably clean and accessible We can run small pilots without major disruption We are comfortable with change and training We can measure time saved or cycle-time reductions Leadership is aligned on ROI goals We have a budget for implementation and support

Interpret your score

40-50: Ready to implement and scale 30-39: Ready for a focused pilot 20-29: Start with assessment and training Below 20: Clarify workflows and data first

We can run this assessment with your team and build a 90-day roadmap.
