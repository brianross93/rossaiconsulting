Avoid These 3 AI Implementation Mistakes

Most AI failures are not technical. They happen because the workflow, data, or adoption plan is missing.

Mistake 1: Starting with the tool instead of the workflow

Teams buy software and then wonder where it fits. Start by mapping the workflow and identifying bottlenecks.

Mistake 2: No adoption plan

AI only works if people use it. Training, prompts, and accountability matter.

Mistake 3: Ignoring data quality

Inconsistent data leads to inconsistent AI. Clean inputs and templates create reliable outputs.

We help Tulsa teams avoid these pitfalls with a structured assessment and rollout plan.
